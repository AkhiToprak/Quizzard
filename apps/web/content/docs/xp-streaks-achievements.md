Notemage has a lightweight progression system layered on top of your study habits. It exists to make showing up every day feel rewarding, not to gamify learning into a grind. There's nothing to grind — points are tied to real study actions.

## XP

You earn XP whenever you do something productive in the app. Concrete amounts:

| Action                      | XP  |
| --------------------------- | --- |
| Send a message in Mage Chat | 5   |
| Edit a page                 | 3   |
| Create a page               | 10  |
| Upload a document           | 15  |
| Review a flashcard          | 2   |
| Complete a flashcard set    | 25  |
| Complete a quiz             | 20  |
| Get a perfect quiz score    | 50  |

Plus streak milestones (see below).

### Levels

XP rolls up into levels on a curve — each level needs more XP than the last (the curve grows by ~1.5x per level). You'll see your current level and the bar to the next one in your profile.

## Streaks

Your **streak** counts consecutive days you've been active in the app. Any of the XP-earning actions above counts as activity for that day. Streaks are calculated in UTC, so the day boundary doesn't shift around when you travel.

### Streak freezes

You start with **2 streak freezes**. If you miss a day with a freeze in your inventory, the freeze gets used automatically and your streak survives. Run out of freezes and miss a day → the streak resets to 0.

The freeze is a safety net for the day life gets in the way, not a way to play one day a week.

### Milestone bonuses

Hitting a streak length awards a one-time XP bonus:

- **7 days** → +100 XP
- **30 days** → +500 XP
- **100 days** → +2000 XP

## Achievements

There are 18 achievements in total, grouped into five categories:

- **Study** — for hitting study goals (e.g. _Daily goal_).
- **Content** — for creating things in your notebooks.
- **Social** — for friends and groups (e.g. _First friend_, _First group_).
- **Streak** — for streak milestones (e.g. _7-day streak_).
- **Special** — including a meta achievement, _All achievements_, that unlocks once you've collected the other 17.

Each achievement shows your progress toward it (e.g. "5 / 7 days") so you can see what's next without guessing.

## Where to see it all

Your profile page shows your current level, total XP, current streak, longest streak, and unlocked achievements. The dashboard surfaces today's streak and a "you earned X XP" toast as you go.
