# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our user interface.

## Branding & Colors

**Color Palette:** Our brand uses a sophisticated and energetic palette, anchored by a deep purple (`#8c52ff`) as the primary hue. A vibrant yellow (`#ffde59`) serves as a dynamic secondary accent. The interface operates in a `dark` mode, utilizing a near-black (`#0D0D1A`) for neutral backgrounds and surfaces, ensuring strong contrast and a modern feel.

## Typography

**Headlines:** All headlines are set in `Space Grotesk`, a modern geometric sans-serif font that conveys a sense of tech-forward innovation and clarity.
**Body Text & Labels:** For all body text and UI labels, we use `Epilogue`. This font is chosen for its excellent readability and contemporary yet approachable character, ensuring a consistent and pleasant reading experience across the interface.

## Shape & Form

**Roundedness:** UI elements feature `moderately rounded` corners (a roundedness value of 2). This provides a friendly and modern aesthetic without appearing overly soft or pill-shaped, striking a balance between sharpness and approachability.

## Spacing

**Density:** The spacing within and between UI elements is `normal` (a spacing value of 2). This provides a balanced layout that is neither too dense nor too spacious, ensuring clear information hierarchy and comfortable interaction without feeling cluttered or spread out.
